Attributes

data-tc-email

Paypal Email address to checkout

data-tc-currency

Currency used in your cart. (EUR or USD)

DEFAULT: EUR

data-tc-shipping-free-since

Free shipment if the client expend at least this value

DEFAULT: 10000

data-tc-shipping-cost

Standard Shiping cost based on currency

DEFAULT: 15

data-tc-tax-rate

Standard tax rate

DEFAULT: 0.21
