Attributes

data-tc-items

The number of items you want to see on the screen.

DEFAULT: 1

data-tc-nav

Show next/prev buttons.

DEFAULT: true

data-tc-margin

margin-right(px) on item

DEFAULT: 10

data-tc-video

Enable fetching YouTube/Vimeo videos.

DEFAULT: true

data-tc-video-width

Width of the videos.

DEFAULT: 100%

data-tc-video-height

Height of the videos.

DEFAULT: 300

data-tc-center

Center item. Works well with even an odd number of items.

DEFAULT: true

data-tc-autoplay

Autoplay

DEFAULT: true

data-tc-device-items

The number of items you want to see on the screen separated by coma. First number is on mobile, second on tablet and third in desktop.

DEFAULT: 1,1,1
